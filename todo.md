# ALLSMILES Academy Facebook Promotional Slides

## Design Guidelines

### Design References
- **Primary Style**: Bright, Educational, Family-Friendly
- **Color Palette**: Warm, inviting colors that appeal to parents and children
- **Mood**: Energetic, trustworthy, professional yet approachable

### Color Palette
- Primary: #FF6B6B (Coral Red - energetic, friendly)
- Secondary: #4ECDC4 (Turquoise - trust, learning)
- Accent: #FFE66D (Sunny Yellow - optimism, happiness)
- Background: #F7F7F7 (Light Gray - clean)
- Text: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue-Gray - professional)

### Typography
- Heading1: Plus Jakarta Sans font-weight 700 (56px) - Main titles
- Heading2: Plus Jakarta Sans font-weight 600 (40px) - Section titles
- Heading3: Plus Jakarta Sans font-weight 600 (28px) - Subtitles
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans font-weight 400 (18px) - Normal text
- Emphasis: Plus Jakarta Sans font-weight 700 (18px) - Highlighted text

### Key Component Styles
- **Cards**: Light backgrounds with subtle shadows, rounded corners (rounded-2xl)
- **Buttons**: Bright accent colors with hover effects
- **Icons**: Playful, educational themed

### Images to Generate
1. **hero-online-learning-kids.jpg** - Happy diverse children learning on tablets/laptops at home, bright natural lighting, smiling faces (Style: photorealistic, warm atmosphere)
2. **curriculum-books-colorful.jpg** - Colorful educational books, notebooks, pencils arranged beautifully, top-down view (Style: photorealistic, vibrant colors)
3. **teacher-student-online.jpg** - Friendly teacher on video call with engaged student, professional home office setup (Style: photorealistic, professional yet warm)
4. **family-learning-together.jpg** - Parent and children learning together at home, laptop open, happy expressions (Style: photorealistic, cozy home environment)
5. **graduation-success-celebration.jpg** - Child celebrating academic achievement, diploma/certificate, proud moment (Style: photorealistic, joyful atmosphere)
